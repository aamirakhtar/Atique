-Polymorphism:-
Same name of the property or function/method, but different Behaviours.

1-Dynamic Polymorphism (Overriding):-
Inheritance is a must.
Specialization.

2-Static Polymorphism (Overloading):-
Inheritance is not must.

Rules:-
a-Number of the parameters.
b-Type fo the parameters
c-order of the type fo the parameters
